Yaoxuan Wu

Hi, all! I'm Yaoxuan (Thaddy) Wu, a third-year CS PhD at UCLA. I'm fortunate to work with Prof. Miryung Kim. I am generally interested in program analysis and testing. My current research focuses on software testing for big data analytics.


Prior to joining UCLA, I completed my undergrad in Turing Class, Peking University. During my undergrad years, I was also fortunate to work with Dr. Xin Zhang at Peking University.
